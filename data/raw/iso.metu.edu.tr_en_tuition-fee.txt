Tuition Fee Exemptions
Please note that the following groups of students are exempted from tuition fee payments as long as they are within their regular period of study:
International students with the Republic of Türkiye nationality (only those who started their programs in the 2020-2021 Spring Semester or before)
Students with Syrian nationality (only those who started their programs in the 2020-2021 Spring Semester or before)
Students with blue card -
Mavi Kart
(only those who started their programs in the 2020-2021 Spring Semester or before)
International students with the TRNC nationality who studied high school in TRNC
Students with a scholarship (that covers the tuition fee) from their country of origin or another institution
Undergraduate students who have graduated from high schools listed in
this link
.
Tuition Fee Discount
Following Article 3(2) of the 7384 numbered Presidential Decision, students who satisfy all of the below-listed conditions will be provided a %25 tuition fee discount:
If you started your program at METU in the 2023-2024 Academic Year or later
If you are not registered in GIA, BAS, TEFL, GETMA programs, or in any evening program for a Master’s degree
If your family (at least one of your parents) legally resides in Türkiye with a residence permit (i.e., you and your family members should have a foreign ID number that starts with 9)
If another member of your family is also a registered/active student in another Turkish public university
If you are not already given a tuition fee discount or exemption at METU higher than %25 for any other reason
Before the start of each semester, those students who would like to apply for this discount should combine the below-listed documents into one PDF file and then send it to the International Students Office via email (
iso@metu.edu.tr
):
Petition – Signed and scanned
Proof of residence permit via
https://turkiye.gov.tr/goc-idaresi-ikamet-izni-kisisel-bilgi-sorgulama-s...
: (i) for the METU student, (ii) for at least one of the parents and (iii) for the other member of the family who studies in another Turkish public university (just a copy of the residence permit card will not be accepted)
Proof of registration in another Turkish public university: Student certificate downloaded via E-Devlet showing that the other member of the family is also a registered/active student in another Turkish public university
*%25 tuition fee discounts can only be given for the fall and spring semesters. It does not apply to summer school fees.
