METU is Turkey’s premier state university rated among the top world universities in
several ﬁelds/rankings over the years:
THE: 85th in the top 400 universities list of
THE “World University Rankings 2014-2015”
QS: Repeatedly among top universities in the world in 19 subject areas under

Red Dot Design Ranking: Repeatedly 5th among the
“Most Successful Design Schools” of Americas and Europe
Recipient of Turkish Higher Education Council’s (YÖK) Excellence in Academia Award for

Over 2,000 international students from 98 diﬀerent countries studying toward
a myriad of academic degrees.
2,000+ international undergraduate students apply each year with 13% acceptance rate.

METU/ODTÜ as a prominent research university:
METU has actively taken part in and managed various projects under the Instrument for
Pre-Accession Assistance (IPA), especially the Framework Programmes (FP) as well as
other international projects, such as COST, Erasmus, Newton-Katip Çelebi Fund, etc.
Outstanding research centers: Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems Research and
Application Center (MEMS), Center of Excellence in Biomaterials and Tissue Engineering
(BIOMATEN), Center for Solar Energy Research and Applications (GÜNAM),
Center for Black Sea and Central Asia (KORA), etc.
Over 25% of all students enrolled in graduate programs and 270+ PhD degrees awarded
each year.
Competitive research revenues accounting to 20-25% on average of all
METU expenditures annually (including all payroll costs).
Research budget: 350+ international projects with a total budget of 760M €
with a METU share of 50M €.
307 completed international R&D projects with a total budget of about 639M € and
a METU share of 40M € (156 of these projects were EU Framework Programmes projects
with a total budget of 561M €, within which METU share was 31M €.)
Either the coordinator or beneﬁciary of 30 projects with a total budget of 130M € and
a METU share of 9M €, out of which 23 are ongoing or in grant agreement phase.
In terms of national R&D projects, where TÜBİTAK (The Scientiﬁc and Technological
Research Council of Turkey) is the leading funding agency, METU has a prominent
history amongst Turkish universities with more than 1,100+ projects having a total
budget of 720M TL (approx. 110M €).
Top public university in TÜBİTAK’s Entrepreneur and Innovative University Index 2017.
METU’s Technology Transfer Ofﬁce (TTO): 150+ inventions, 20+ commercialized patents,
300+ patent applications, 200+ granted patents and more than 8M TL commercialized
income.
Based on a total closed area of 170,000 m2 and 30 facility buildings,
ODTÜ TEKNOKENT currently houses 380+ companies (60% of which were start-ups),
6,500+ R&D personnel and 1,200+ projects.
Its goal is to provide contemporary infrastructure and superstructure to researchers
and companies that mutually develop and produce the technologies. With its activities
to facilitate university-industry collaboration, ODTÜ TEKNOKENT is the catalyst for
synergy between the parties.
It has ranked 1st for six times in a row in the Technology Development Zones
Performance Index study of the Ministry of Industry and Technology realized since 2011.
To date 1.4+ billion US dollars in technology exports (not including manufacturing
numbers) and 13+ billion TL in technology sales realized via ODTÜ TEKNOKENT.
