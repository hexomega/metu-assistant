The application period for international undergraduate programs at METU Ankara Campus for the 2025–2026 academic year has now ended. Applicants who have submitted their applications can track their status via the online application portal. We strongly recommend checking your email inbox regularly, as important updates regarding your application may be shared.
Admission decisions are expected to be finalized in the second week of August.
