METU offers the following support options for university students in Ukraine:

2. EXCHANGE (MOBILITY) STUDIES
3. FLEXIBLE ADMISSION TO UNDERGRADUATE DEGREE PROGRAMS (see below please)

.
Important note about the application dates:
Students from Ukraine who will take the OZN exam in July 2022 and who are planning to apply for the undergraduate degree programs should follow the below-given steps:
- Visit
https://oibs2.metu.edu.tr/Application_for_International_Students_to_Unde...
and create an account by clicking "new application" button
- Login with your username and account, and then start your application.
- In the "Exam Result" field, select "Ukraine - Ukrainian External Independent Test-OZN" and upload a document/screenshot that shows that you will take the OZN exam in July 2022. Enter your predicted score to the score field.
- Fill out the other required fields in the application form and freeze your application.
The dealine to freeze the form is the 22nd of July 2022. Even if the OZN exam and the result release date will be after this deadline, you have to submit your application online by
the 22nd of July 2022
.
- Once the OZN results are out, please send your results to
iso@metu.edu.tr
Do not forget to state your application number in your email.
