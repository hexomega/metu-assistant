team via:
E-mail:

Phone
: +90 312 210 7171
Online Meeting:
https://calendly.com/study-metu
2. For Current, Newly Accepted, or Alumni International Students
If you are a current, newly accepted or alumni international student, please contact the
International Students Office (ISO)
via:
E-mail:
iso[at]metu.edu.tr
Phone
: +90 312 210 3414 / 4181
Online Meeting:
https://calendly.com/metu_iso
Walk-In Office Hours:
Fridays, 13:30–16:00 / Location: MM Building, 4th Floor, Room 424 (Please use the remote service options above before visiting in person.)
Paperwork Drop-Off & Collection
Official documents (student certificate, transcript, educational certificate, student ID cards, diploma, etc.) are issued only by the Registrar's Office, not the ISO.
Student certificates, transcripts, and educational certificates are also available electronically. More information: See the "Documents" menu at
https://oidb.metu.edu.tr/en/
Residence Permit Information Forms (Expected Date of Graduation Document) are also provided only in electronic format. Details:
https://iso.metu.edu.tr/en/residence-permit
To submit a petition regarding an official request/complaint, please visit the Documentation Unit in the Presidency Building, -1 Floor.
