(1) Su
bmissions of Exam Grades during “Intensive Online Education Term”
a. The course grades that have not been submitted during the grade submission period of "Online Education Term" will be displayed as the Incomplete (I) grade on the system.
b. During the “Intensive Online Education Term” grading, the Incomplete (I) grades will be updated by the instructors and converted into letter grades.
c. For the courses to be completed in the “Intensive Online Education Term”,
before the submission of the grades by the instructors, the grades will be displayed on the system as Incomplete (I) grades.
d. For the courses to be completed in the “Intensive Online Education Term”, following the submission of the grades by the instructors,
the students will be able to see their letter grades on the system. Hence, for the
“Intensive Online Education Term”
there will be no extra grade submission and announcement dates.
