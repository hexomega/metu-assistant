Undergraduate Programs and Degrees
The program duration is four years (eight semesters), and this duration is five years (ten semesters) for programs offering a master’s degree along with an undergraduate degree.
The maximum duration of an undergraduate program is seven years (fourteen semesters), which is nine years (sixteen semesters) for programs offering a master’s degree along with an undergraduate degree.
The minimum total ECTS credits required to graduate from associate degree and undergraduate programs are as follows.
Associate degree programs (Industrial Electronic Program) 120 ECTS;
Undergraduate programs 240 ECTS;
International Joint Degree programs; Global and International Affair and Business Administration 120 ECTS, English Language Teaching 180 ECTS;
5-year non-thesis master programs (Physics and Chemistry Education Programs) 300 ECTS.
Undergraduate programs are carried out by faculties. ODTÜ has 5 faculties and the degrees offered are listed below:
