On 25th of January 2023, an information program held at METU Culture and Congress Center by the Ankara Provincial Directorate of Migration Management for our international students. The program included sessions about the social cohesion in Turkey and the practical information regarding the student residence permits. Around 300 international students participated the event and deserved to collect their certificates of participation.
