There have been some additions/updates in the application criteria list, which can be used by the international students in their application to the undergraduate programs at METU Ankara and Northern Cyprus Campuses. Changes are as follows:
Ankara Campus
Botswana
: A minimum grade of A in at least five subjects, two of which are related to the program applied for, in the Botswana General Certificate of Secondary Education (BGCSE).
Burundi
: A minimum score of 90% in Burundi Diplôme des Humanités Complètes
Democratic Republic of Congo
: A minimum score of 90% in the Diplôme d'Etat d'Etudes Secondaires du Cycle Long in the chosen stream (section)
Indonesia
: A minimum graduation score of 90% in the high school graduation exams or a minimum score of 620 in the related field of UTBK exam
Ukraine
: A minimum average grade of 160 out of 200 in at least two subjects relevant to the program applied for, in the Ukrainian “External Independent Test (OZN)”.
Northern Cyprus Campus
Botswana
: A minimum grade of C in at least five subjects, two of which are related to the program applied for, in the Botswana General Certificate of Secondary Education (BGCSE).
Burundi
: A minimum score of 70% in Burundi Diplôme des Humanités Complètes
Democratic Republic of Congo
: A minimum score of 60% in the Diplôme d'Etat d'Etudes Secondaires du Cycle Long in the chosen stream (section)
Indonesia
: A minimum graduation score of 80% in the high school graduation exams or a minimum score of 590 in the related field of UTBK exam
Ukraine
: A minimum average grade of 145 out of 200 in at least two subjects relevant to the program applied for, in the Ukrainian "External Independent Test (OZN)".
