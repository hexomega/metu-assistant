Applications for the TR-YÖS exam, which is one of the exams that can be used in the international student applications for the undergraduate programs at METU, have started. We would like to remind the important dates for those who will use TR-YÖS as the primary application document or those who will use an exam/diploma other than TR-YÖS as the primary application document but want to attach TR-YÖS score as an additional document.
Exam date:
18 May 2024
Application dates: 1-18 March 2024
Late application dates: 26-28 March 2024
Detailed information about the exam*:
https://www.osym.gov.tr/TR,29233/2024-tr-yos1-kilavuz-ve-basvuru-bilgile...
*Please contact ÖSYM, Centre for Assessment, Selection and Placement, for your questions regarding the exam, using the methods given in the exam manual.
