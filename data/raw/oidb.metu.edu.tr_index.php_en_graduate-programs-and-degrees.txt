Graduate Programs and Degrees
Graduate programs are administered by Graduate Schools. ODTÜ has five institutes. When the conditions are met based on the legislation, a master's/doctorate degree is obtained. Some departments have options for a Master's with a thesis and/or without a thesis.
The conditions for completion of graduate programs are specified in the METU Rules and Regulations Governing Graduate Studies. The minimum course load and credits of graduate programs based on legislation are mentioned below. However, accordingly, to the Higher Education Institute Rules and Regulations for Graduate Programs, program curriculums determined by the related institute board may differ for each graduate program.
