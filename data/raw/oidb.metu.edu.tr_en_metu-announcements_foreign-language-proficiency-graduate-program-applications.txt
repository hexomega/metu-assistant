Foreign Language Proficiency in Graduate Program Applications
Dear Students,
In accordance with the decision of the Middle East Technical University Senate dated 11 November 2025, it has been decided that candidates applying to graduate programs who, as of the date of application, fall within the last four semesters:
a. have graduated from an undergraduate or graduate program of our University, or
b. have held student status in one of our University's graduate programs for at  least one semester, provided that they meet the EPE score specified  by the Graduate School to which they are applying,
will not be required to resubmit proof of English proficiency.
