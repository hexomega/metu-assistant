Applications for the TR-YÖS exam, which is one of the exams that can be used in the international student applications for the undergraduate programs at METU, will soon start. We would like to remind the important dates for those who will use TR-YÖS as the primary application document or those who will use an exam/diploma other than TR-YÖS as the primary application document but want to attach TR-YÖS score as an additional document.
The exam will be held in 52 different countries and in Turkish, German, Arabic, French, English and Russian languages.
Exam date:
11 May 2025
Application dates: 13 February-12 March 2025
Late application dates: 18-20 March 2025
The exam manual will be announced by ÖSYM soon:
https://www.osym.gov.tr/
and
https://tryos.osym.gov.tr/TryosYetki/Giris
*Please contact ÖSYM, Centre for Assessment, Selection and Placement, for your questions regarding the exam, using the methods given in the exam manual.
