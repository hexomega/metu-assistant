TEKNOFEST TRNC TECHNOLOGY COMPETITIONS APPLICATIONS ARE NOW OPEN!
Dear Students,
TEKNOFEST Technology Competitions, focusing on the technologies of the future, are set to unite technology and innovation enthusiasts in 2025 in the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus.
TEKNOFEST TRNC, being held in the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus, aims to bring innovations to the technology world with its inaugural competitions. The application process has started for the TEKNOFEST Technology Competitions, set to create significant excitement in the world of technology and innovation with events such as the inaugural TEKNOFEST Roboleague Blue Homeland Competition, TEKNOFEST TRNC Research Project Competition, HackMasters Blue Homeland competition, and many others, all happening for the first time in 2025. In the TEKNOFEST Technology Competitions, where participants have the opportunity to showcase and develop their projects, the top entries will receive a total of ₺2.5 million in prizes and ₺3 million in financial support.
Apply to the TEKNOFEST TRNC Technology Competitions, which will host participants from all around the world, and shape the technologies of the future!
Application Deadline: January 15, 2025
