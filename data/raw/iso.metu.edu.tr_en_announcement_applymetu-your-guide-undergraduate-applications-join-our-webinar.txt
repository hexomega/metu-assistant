The Middle East Technical University (ODTÜ/METU) Ankara Campus will open its undergraduate application period for international students on June 2, 2025, for the 2025–2026 academic year.
To support prospective applicants, we are hosting a live webinar just before the application period begins. We warmly invite international students, as well as their parents, counselors, and teachers, to join us for this informative session.
Date: May 30, 2025
Time: 14:30 (Türkiye time)
Language: English
Format: Live online webinar
CLICK HERE TO REGISTER
What to Expect:
An overview of Middle East Technical University (ODTÜ/METU)
Introduction to our faculties and undergraduate programs
A step-by-step explanation of the application process
Live Q&A session to answer your questions
Whether you're planning to apply or simply exploring your options, this session will provide essential guidance on becoming a part of METU's international community.
We look forward to meeting you online!
