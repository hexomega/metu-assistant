Mission, Vision and Values
Our Mission,
Ensuring efficacy, sustainability, efficiency and satisfaction in services and processes; providing students, academic and administrative units and external stakeholders with accurate, innovative, quick, perfect and current information; sustaining our status of being a pioneer and model “Student Affairs Office” at the national level by rendering our services in a manner that supports the METU vision.
Our Vision,
Rendering our services to our stakeholders at international standards.
Our Values,
Student Affairs employed exemplary stuffs who aware of the limits of knowledge, ability and authority in fulfillment of the responsibilities and the solution of the problems encountered, have adopted hard working, solidarity, trustworthy, teamwork principled, scientific, technological and ethical working rules, unbiased, equitable, self-improved , proud of the institution in which they work.
