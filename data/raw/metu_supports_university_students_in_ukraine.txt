IN UKRAINE

1. SUMMER INTERNSHIP
A. Internship Slots:
•
International Cooperations Office: Manages the University’s international ties with
institutions abroad and student & staff mobility (exchange) programs. www.ico.metu.edu.tr
•
International Students Office: Manages the University’s international student admissions.
www.iso.metu.edu.tr

B. Duration: 2 or 3 months between July – September 2022 (min. duration 2 months). Students must
complete the min. duration of 2 months in order to be able to receive funding.

C. Application Criteria: Applicants must meet the below criteria and present related
certificates/documents during their application to METU.

Application Criteria
Required Certificate/Document
Being enrolled at a university in Ukraine
(bachelor, master or PhD level)
Student certificate approved by the home
university in Ukraine
Having good command of English (min.
B2 level)
English proficiency document from the
home university or any language centre
Being interested in pursuing an internship
experience in the field of
internationalisation at the university level
Letter of intent (300 words max.)

D. Funding: Monthly support and travel support will be available under Erasmus+ program.
It is also possible for students to do their internship without receiving grant.

E. Accommodation and Other Facilities: Students will be offered accommodation in campus
dormitories on a modest fee. They will also be able to benefit from campus facilities such as the
library, cafeteria, etc.

F. Application: Please send an e-mail to kgulizar@metu.edu.tr to receive more information about
application documents. Application deadline is July 17th, 2022.

F. Contact: kgulizar@metu.edu.tr

2. EXCHANGE (MOBILITY) STUDIES
This option gives students to be enrolled at METU for one semester and to receive residence permit
(study permit) in Turkey for the whole duration. At the end of the semester students will be given a
transcript of records showing the list and grades of courses they have taken at METU.

The list of academic programs available for exchange studies can be found in the list in Section D
below. Students are advised to check the compatibility of their program in Ukraine with METU.

A. Duration: Fall semester 2022 (end of September – end of January 2022). Students must complete
the whole semester in order to be able to receive funding.

B. Application Criteria: Applicants must meet the below criteria and present related certificates/
documents during their application to METU.

C. Offered Courses: Students will be offered to take courses under the following faculties & graduate
schools. Please click on the list items for further information on available departments and courses.
• Faculty of Architecture
• Faculty of Arts and Sciences
• Faculty of Economic and Administrative Sciences
• Faculty of Education
• Faculty of Engineering
• Graduate School of Applied Mathematics
• Graduate School of Informatics
• Graduate School of Marine Sciences
• Graduate School of Natural and Applied Sciences
• Graduate School of Social Sciences

D. Funding: Monthly support and travel support will be available under Erasmus+ program.
It is also possible for students to participate in the exchange program without receiving grant.

E. Accommodation and Other Facilities: Students will be offered accommodation in campus
dormitories on a modest fee. They will also be able to benefit from campus facilities such as the
library, cafeteria, etc.

F. Application: Please send an e-mail to kgulizar@metu.edu.tr to receive more information about
application documents. Application deadline is July 17th, 2022.

G. Contact: kgulizar@metu.edu.tr

3. FLEXIBLE ADMISSION TO UNDERGRADUATE DEGREE PROGRAMS
3.1. Freshman Applications:
•
Detailed information is available at https://iso.metu.edu.tr
•
The list of the programs with international student quotas:
https://iso.metu.edu.tr/en/undergraduate-programs-international-students
•
Online application dates: July 4-22, 2022 (for the 2022-2022 Fall Semester)
•
Opportunity to apply using Ukrainian national OZN exam (taken within the last two years and
with a minimum average grade of 160 out of 200 in at least two OZN subjects relevant to the
program applied for.
•
Tuition fees are available at
http://oidb.metu.edu.tr/sites/oidb.metu.edu.tr/files/2021_2022_INTL_STUD_TUITION.pdf
(with a possibility of fee waivers and discounts)
•
Upon admission and registration, opportunity to transfer your previous courses and credits
from your previous university to your new program at METU (in line with METU’s regulations
and the academic evaluation of the department)
3.2. Transfer Applications:
•
Detailed information is available at http://oidb.metu.edu.tr/en/transfer-announcement-0 and
http://oidb.metu.edu.tr/en/middle-east-technical-university-directive-student-transfers-
undergraduate
•
Transfer application dates: July 4-22, 2022 (for the 2022-2022 Fall Semester)
•
Application requirements:
o In the year when you registered to your current Ukrainian university, having a
minimum average grade of 180 out of 200 in at least two OZN subjects relevant to the
program applied for.
o English Proficiency Document that satisfies the minimum scores stated at this link for
exemption from the Department of Basic English (Preparatory School) program:
http://oidb.metu.edu.tr/en/equivalence-table-english-language-exams-recognized-
metu-undergraduate-and-graduate-students
o Applicants must have completed at least two semesters in their current program
o Applicants must have taken and passed all the required courses in the curriculum of
their current program within the completed semesters.
o The Cumulative GPA of applicants must be minimum 2.50/4.00
•
Tuition fees are available at
http://oidb.metu.edu.tr/sites/oidb.metu.edu.tr/files/2021_2022_INTL_STUD_TUITION.pdf
(with a possibility of fee waivers and discounts)
•
Upon admission and registration, opportunity to transfer your previous courses and credits
from your previous university to your new program at METU (in line with METU’s regulations
and the academic evaluation of the department)
