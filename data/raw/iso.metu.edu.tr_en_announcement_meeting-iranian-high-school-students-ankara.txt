On 28
th
of February, 2019, our international student advisors had a visit to the 22 Bahman Iranian School in Ankara and met with the high school students who are willing to apply to our university. Students have been informed about the Middle East Technical University in general and about the application criteria as an international student.
On 6
th
of March, 2019, these students also had a visit to our university and had a chance to experience the student life on our Ankara campus.
We would like to express our thanks to Mr. Mohammadi Pour and Mr. Ghaffar Abdollahi for their warm welcome and hospitality, and also to our student Daniyal Vahabli (PHYS-1st year) for his companionship.
