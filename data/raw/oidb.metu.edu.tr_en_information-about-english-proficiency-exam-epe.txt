Information about the English Proficiency Exam (EPE)
Application announcements and all other information regarding the English Proficiency Exam (EPE) to be held by our university's School of Foreign Languages are announced on the web address
https://epe.metu.edu.tr/
.
For all your questions and problems regarding the English Proficiency Exam, you can contact
epe@metu.edu.tr
e-mail address.
The application dates for the Exam Periods are specified in the Academic Calendar (
https://oidb.metu.edu.tr/tr/akademik-takvim
).
Results can be seen on the following pages:
For active students:
https://student.metu.edu.tr
.
For METU students applying outside of METU or inactive students:
Applications for the English Proficiency Exam (Program 238) can be found at
https://application.metu.edu.tr
. Academic (Institutes and Head of Departments) and administrative units can use METUSIS and follow the steps
Authorized Processes> Lists> English Exam Score
to see the exam scores.
NOTE:
Exam result certificates are not provided to be submitted to any unit within METU. Those who want to obtain a document to be submitted outside METU can apply to the Registrar's Office.
