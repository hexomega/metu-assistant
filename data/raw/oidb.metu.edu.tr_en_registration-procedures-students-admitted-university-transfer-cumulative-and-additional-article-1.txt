REGISTRATION PROCEDURES FOR STUDENTS ADMITTED TO THE UNIVERSITY WITH TRANSFER (CUMULATIVE AND ADDITIONAL ARTICLE 1 )
Procedures to be done by the students who will make a final registration through the Transfer Program
Visit
https://useraccount.metu.edu.tr/newstudent/
to take the user code and password
Visit
http://oidb.metu.edu.tr/en/student-id-cards
to get detailed information about id card processes and apply for a new id card over
https://cardinfo.metu.edu.tr
.
Students who have been admitted under Additional Article 1 and exceeded the maximum duration of education are required to pay a tuition fee before the interactive registrations. They can visit
http://oidb.metu.edu.tr/en/tuition-fee-amounts
to see the tuition fee amounts.
For interactive registrations and advisor approvals, students are required to select courses and get advisor approval by following the web address
https://student.metu.edu.tr/
between the dates specified in the Academic Calendar.
The Basic English Unit will administer a placement test for students admitted to the Preparatory Class under Additional Article 1.
Students can find more information at
https://dbe.metu.edu.tr
. Those who are required to study in the preparatory class for one year must pay a tuition fee. Tuition amounts can be found at
http://oidb.metu.edu.tr/en/tuition-fee-amounts
.
Scholarship Applications
Students who wish to benefit from the scholarships distributed under the organization of the METU Scholarship and Aid Committee can review the information on
https://oidb.metu.edu.tr/tr/burs-ve-yardim-basvurulari
and apply to the Student Affairs Department/Scholarship Office.
Dormitory Applications
Students who want to benefit from the dormitories on METU campus can get detailed information from the
http://yurtlar.metu.edu.tr/
web address of METU Dormitories
