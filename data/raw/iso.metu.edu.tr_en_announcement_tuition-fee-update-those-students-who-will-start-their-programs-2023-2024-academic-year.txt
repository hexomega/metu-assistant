With the University Administrative Board decision dated 31.01.2023, the tuition fees for those international students who will start their programs in ODTÜ Ankara and Erdemli Campuses in the 2023-2024 Academic Year Fall Semester or later have been updated.
The updated fees are available here.
The fees for the students who have started their programs in the 2022-2023 spring semester or before have not been updated yet. The updated fees for these students will be announced after the related Turkish Presidency decision is announced within 2023.
