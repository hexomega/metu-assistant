Announcement for Unit of Basic English Students to Submit English Language Certificate (Additional Document) (For students who will take courses from undergraduate programs in 2025-2026 Fall Semester)
Students who fail at the end of the first or second year in the Unit of Basic English Preparatory Class for academic reasons, who have the right to submit an internationally valid document (TOEFL IBT, IELTS, and PTE) accepted by the University Senate;
The students are supposed to send their English Language Exam scores and the English Language Certificate submission form available on
http://oidb.metu.edu.tr/tr/formlar
as an e-mail to the
oidb-tib@metu.edu.tr
between
01 - 23 September 2025
.  Students who want to submit their documents by hand must contact the Department of Basic English advisor in the Registrar's office.
A notification e-mail will be sent to students who submit their documents via e-mail after their documents are processed. Students who receive an information e-mail must complete the Interactive registration and advisor approvals (Associate, undergraduate, and graduate programs) between
24 - 26 September 2025
.
You can reach the equivalence table of English Proficiency Exams accepted by METU from the address given below.
http://oidb.metu.edu.tr/en/equivalence-table-english-language-exams-recognized-metu-undergraduate-and-graduate-students
