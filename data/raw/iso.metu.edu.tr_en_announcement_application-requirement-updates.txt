With the University Senate Decision dated 05/01/2024, the list of the exams and qualifications that can be used as the primary application document for undergraduate programs at METU Ankara Campus and also the minimum scores required for each of these qualifications have been updated. The updated list is available via this link:
https://iso.metu.edu.tr/en/system/files/odtu_iso_requirements.pdf
The international student applications for undergraduate programs at METU Ankara Campus will be received and evaluated in line with this updated list as of the applications for the 2024-2025 academic year.
