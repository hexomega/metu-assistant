APPLICATION PROCESS
Double Major and Minor applications will be done under the title of
DOUBLE MAJOR/MINOR APPLICATIONS
given on the following web page
https://application.metu.edu.tr/
Candidates should enter the link above with their user code and password.
Candidates should mark their preferences among the listed major/minor programs (order of the preferences should be considered). Maximum 5 programs can be selected.
There will be no document submission (transcript, application form) to Registrar’s Office.
ANNOUNCEMENT OF RESULTS OF PRE-EVALUATION AND EVALUATION
Phase I: Pre-evaluation
The applicants are evaluated depending on the application procedures stated in the related regulation. Applications of the candidates, fulfilling the requirements, are submitted to the related academic units. Applications of the candidates who do not fulfill the requirements are “rejected” during the application process.
After the pre-evaluation, students who are not accepted due to not satisfying application requirements are informed via email.
Phase II: Evaluation
Candidates who satisfied the application criteria and whose application was evaluated affirmative by the related academic units are announced on the following webpage:
https://application.metu.edu.tr/
(
Application for Double Major and Minor Program
).
If a student applies for both transfer and major/minor programs at the same time and gets admission for transfer, then his application for major/minor programs is not evaluated.
