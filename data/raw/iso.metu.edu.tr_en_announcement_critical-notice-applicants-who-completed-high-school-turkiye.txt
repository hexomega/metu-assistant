This announcement concerns all applicants who completed their high school education in Türkiye, regardless of their nationality, and who intend to apply through the international student quotas, in line with the most recent decisions and regulations issued by the Council of Higher Education (YÖK).
For detailed and binding information about your eligibility to apply through international student quotas for the 2026-2027 academic year, please refer to the official website below:
