ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System
METU Registrar's Office has been qualified to obtain ISO 9001:2008 Quality Management System Certificate on December 27, 2013, and ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System Certificate on December 20, 2017.
In accordance with the ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System standards,  the present Quality Management System is updated and has been switched to ISO 9001:2015 standards,
Through the Quality Management System, our aim is to provide products and services, meet user demands and terms of legal regulations that are in effect, and execute in accordance with the quality system.
