Graduate Programs
APPLICATION PROCEDURE
The relevant Graduate School Directorates carry out graduate program applications and admissions. Candidates who will apply for graduate programs can reach out to the announcements of the Graduate Schools they will be applying to from the web addresses given below.
Graduate School of Natural and Applied Sciences:
https://fbe.metu.edu.tr/
Graduate School of Social Sciences:
https://sbe.metu.edu.tr/
Graduate School of Marine Sciences:
https://ims.metu.edu.tr/tr
Graduate School of Informatics:
https://ii.metu.edu.tr/
Graduate School of Applied Mathematics:
https://iam.metu.edu.tr/
METU NCC :
https://ncc.metu.edu.tr/tr/
Candidates admitted to graduate programs can access the announcement regarding the document submission procedures at
https://oidb.metu.edu.tr/tr/lisansustu-ogrencilerin-universiteye-kayitlari
after the admission results are announced by the relevant Graduate School Directorates and forwarded to the Office of the Registrar.
