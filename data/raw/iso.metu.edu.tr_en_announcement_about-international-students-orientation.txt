Dear International Students,
We would like to inform you about the International Students Orientation days. You will be welcomed between 24th and 28th of September at Culture and Convention Center (KKM- the building that you all made your registrations).
The program will be started at 9 am. However, the registrations for orientation will be between 8.30 and 8.50. Therefore, please be there before 8.50 since you have to meet your host students.
